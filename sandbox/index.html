  • Interactive Experience

    Learning is no longer a chore

    After all these years we still find ourselves trying to comprehend complex concepts from text. Why make it any harder than it has to be? By utilizing the web to its full potential Aptitude offers something that not many offer. A simple way to learn. See things happen in front of you. Hear the sounds. Understand. Utilize your senses to be able to comprehend what you couldn't before and learn how you individually need to learn.

  • Cross-platform Design

    Available where you need it

    To ensure that you are able to access your subjects from anywhere, Aptitude is a cloud based system. Other companies such as Apple have attempted to make digital textbooks but nothing like this. Accessible from an iPad, a PC, an android tablet; it allows students the flexibility of choosing their hardware so that they don't have to break their backs with heavy textbooks all day.

  • Super Affordable

    You can keep your arm and leg

    Publishing companies have a monopoly on the textbook market. Students' learning and progress isn't really important to them as long as they are selling the next "version". That's in the past. It's not impossible to make textbooks affordable, because that's exactly what we're doing. Making every student happier, smarter, and a little bit richer.

  • Proven Methods

    Modern teaching methods

    There are many methods to teaching and professors each have their own. Each student is different as well, so in order to learn, every semester and in every class the students are the ones that have to adapt to fully learn. Students need to focus more on learning now more than ever so it just makes sense to let them learn in the way that's best for them. Through adaptive learning techniques students can finally grasp concepts even faster through modern techniques.
